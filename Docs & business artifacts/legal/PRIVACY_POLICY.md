# Privacy Policy for FLB Extended

**Last Updated: November 23, 2025**

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
- **Verification Data:** Government-issued ID (NIN, Passport, Driver's License) for identity verification.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the Platform, including logs and device information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the Platform.
- To verify your identity and ensure the safety of our community.
- To process payments and transactions.
- To communicate with you about updates and offers.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Data Sharing
- We do not sell your personal information.
- We may share data with third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors like Interswitch) to facilitate services.
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Data Security
- We use industry-standard security measures (encryption, secure storage) to protect your data.
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
- Verification documents are restricted to authorized personnel only.

## 5. Your Rights
- You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
- You can contact us to exercise these rights.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@flbextended.com.
