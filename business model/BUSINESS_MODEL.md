# FLB Extended — Business Model & Pricing Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
FLB Extended is a comprehensive agricultural platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers, workers, realtors, and modern technology in Nigeria. Our revenue model is a hybrid of transaction fees, subscription services (future), and value-added services.

## 2. Revenue Streams

### A. Transaction Fees (Primary)
We charge a percentage fee on successful transactions facilitated through the platform.
- **Land Leases/Sales:** 1.5% of the total contract value (capped at ₦50,000).
- **Worker Hiring:** 5% of the total labor cost (deducted from the worker's payout or added to the farmer's bill).
- **Produce Sales (Future):** 2.5% of the total sale value.

### B. Wallet & Payment Fees
- **Deposit Fee:** 1.5% (passed to payment processor Interswitch).
- **Withdrawal Fee:** Flat fee of ₦50 per withdrawal to cover bank charges.

### C. Premium Features (Future)
- **Profile Boost:** Farmers and Workers can pay a daily fee (e.g., ₦500/day) to have their profiles featured at the top of search results.
- **Advanced Analytics:** Subscription access to historical price data and advanced crop yield predictions.

## 3. Pricing Structure

| Service | User Cost | Platform Revenue |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Account Creation** | Free | ₦0 |
| **Verification** | Free | ₦0 |
| **Standard Listing** | Free | ₦0 |
| **Featured Listing** | ₦2,000 / week | ₦2,000 |
| **Worker Hiring** | Agreed Rate + 5% | 5% of Rate |
| **Land Lease** | Agreed Rate + 1.5% | 1.5% of Rate |

## 4. Cost Structure
- **Hosting & Infrastructure:** Cloud hosting (AWS/Azure), Database storage.
- **Payment Processing:** Interswitch fees (mostly passed to user).
- **Verification:** Cost of manual admin review (staff time) or API calls to identity verification services.
- **SMS/Email:** Notification costs (Twilio/SendGrid).

## 5. Market Strategy
- **Launch Phase:** Focus on user acquisition by waiving listing fees for the first 3 months.
- **Growth Phase:** Introduce "Featured Listings" and "Verified Pro" badges as paid add-ons.
- **Maturity Phase:** Monetize data insights and bulk-hiring tools for large commercial farms.

## 6. Financial Projections (Year 1)
- **Target Users:** 10,000 (5,000 Farmers, 3,000 Workers, 2,000 Realtors)
- **Projected GMV (Gross Merchandise Value):** ₦500,000,000
- **Projected Revenue:** ₦15,000,000 (approx. 3% blended take rate)
